# Tune down

![](../Images/png/91832843__Web.png)HEXARAY: Level meter

Gives the effect of tuning your guitar lower.

* Use single notes when playing with this effect.

| Parameter | Value | Explanation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| ON/OFF | OFF, ON | Turns this effect on/off. |
| PITCH | -12–0 | Adjusts the pitch in semitones. |

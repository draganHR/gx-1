# Assign

For each parameter, you can specify, in detail, which controller will control which parameter. You can store eight sets of settings for each memory.

1. Press the [MENU] button.
2. ![](../Images/png/330458123__Web.png)
3. Turn the [1] knob to move the cursor to the ASSIGN row.
4. Press the [2] knob.
5. The ASSIGN screen appears.
6. ![](../Images/png/405364619__Web.png)
7. Turn the [1] knob to move the cursor, and turn the [3] knob to set the value.

* Press the [å] [ä] buttons to switch between ASSIGN 1–8.

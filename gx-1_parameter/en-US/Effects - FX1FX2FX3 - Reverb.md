# Reverb

![](../Images/png/91981323__Web.png)HEXARAY: Tempo and stereo level meter

This effect adds reverberation to the sound.

| Parameter | Value | Explanation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| ON/OFF | OFF, ON | Turns this effect on/off. |
| TYPE | HALL S | Simulates the reverberation in a concert hall. Provides clear and spacious reverberations. |
| HALL M | Simulates the reverberation in a concert hall. Provides mild reverberations. |
| PLATE | Simulates plate reverberation (a reverb unit that uses the vibration of a metallic plate). Provides a metallic sound with a distinct upper range. |
| ROOM | Simulates the reverberation in a small room. Provides warm reverberations. |
| STUDIO | Simulates an ambience mic (off-mic, placed at a distance from the sound source) used in recording and other applications. Rather than emphasizing the reverberation, this reverb is used to produce a sense of openness and depth. |
| TIME | 0.1 s–10.0 s | Adjusts the length (time) of reverberation. |
| PRE-DELAY | 0 ms–200 ms | Adjusts the time until the reverb sound starts to output. |
| LEVEL | 0–100 | Adjusts the volume of the reverb sound. |
| DIRECT | 0–100 | Adjusts the volume of the direct sound. |

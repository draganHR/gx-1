# Shimmer

![](../Images/png/91981323__Web.png)

Simulates reverberation with a distinctively sparkling high-frequency range.

| Parameter | Value | Explanation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| ON/OFF | OFF, ON | Turns this effect on/off. |
| TIME | 0.1 s–10.0 s | Adjusts the length (time) of reverberation. |
| TONE | -50–0–+50 | Adjusts the tonal character of the reverb. |
| LEVEL | 0–100 | Adjusts the volume of the reverb sound. |
| PRE-DELAY | 0 ms–200 ms | Adjusts the time until the reverb sound starts to output. |
| PITCH | -24–+24 | Adjusts the amount of pitch shift. |
| PITCH LVL | 0–100 | Adjusts the volume of the pitch shifter. |

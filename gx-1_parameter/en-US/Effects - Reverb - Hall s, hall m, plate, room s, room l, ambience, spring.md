# Hall s, hall m, plate, room s, room l, ambience, spring

![](../Images/png/91981323__Web.png)

| Parameter | Value | Explanation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| ON/OFF | OFF, ON | Turns this effect on/off. |
| TIME | 0.1 s–10.0 s | Adjusts the length (time) of reverberation. |
| TONE | -50–0–+50 | Adjusts the tonal character of the reverb. |
| LEVEL | 1–100 | Adjusts the volume of the reverb sound. |
| DENSITY | 1–10 | Adjusts the density of the reverb sound. |
| PRE-DELAY | 0ms–200ms | Adjusts the time until the reverb sound starts to output. |
| DIRECT | 0–100 | Adjusts the volume of the direct sound. |
